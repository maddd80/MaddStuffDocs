## Key Features

- **🃏 Multi-Player Support**: Up to 4 players can sit at a single table and play against the dealer.
- **🤖 NPC Dealers**: Configurable dealer peds with smooth interaction prompts.
- **📍 Custom Locations**: Easily add tables to any saloon or casino in the world.
- **💰 Configurable Payouts**: Fine-tune the multipliers for Wins, Blackjacks, Flushes, and more.
- **🎨 Custom UI**: A sleek, dark-themed interface for making bets and choosing actions (Hit, Stand, Double).
