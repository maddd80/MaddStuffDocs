## Key Features

- **😀 Floating Emojis**: Show your mood with floating icons above your head.
- **🃏 Tarot Reading**: Fully animated tarot deck with random card draws.
- **🖐️ Rock-Paper-Scissors**: Interactive minigame to settle disputes or just for fun.
- **🎲 Gambling Minigames**: Quick CoinFlip and Dice commands for immediate results.
- **🛠️ High Customizability**: Adjust view distances, durations, and sizes.
