# Madd Grave Robbery

Madd Grave Robbery introduces a high-risk, high-reward criminal activity to your server. Players can desecrate graves to find loot, but must watch out for police alerts and limited working hours.

## Key Features

- **⚰️ Grave Digging**: Use a shovel to dig up specific graves across the map.
- **👮 Police Alerts**: Configurable chance to alert lawmen, creating a radius blip on their map.
- **🌙 Restricted Hours**: Limit grave robbing to specific times (e.g., only at night).
- **📦 Dynamic Loot**: Configurable loot tables with support for items and cash.
- **🤝 NPC Selling**: A built-in system to sell your "found" goods to any citizen NPC.
- **📜 Discord Logs**: Keep track of every grave dug and item sold.
