## Key Features

- **🎵 Portable Music**: Place a phonograph prop anywhere in the world.
- **💿 Record System**: Use specific items as records to play tracks.
- **🛠️ Records Maker**: Create your own custom records at designated shops.
- **🔊 3D Spatial Audio**: Sound fades with distance, just like a real phonograph.
- **🏗️ Edit Mode**: Rotate and position the phonograph perfectly before placing.
